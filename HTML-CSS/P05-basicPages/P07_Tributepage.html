Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)

Based on the stories of Marvel Comics.

The MCU

Iron Man (2008) kicked off the MCU as we know it today, and today it stands with of which The Infinity-Saga consists of 23 movies while The Multiverse-Saga as of now consists of 10 movies and 11 television series with some of them having multiple seasons already.

The Infinity-Saga

The The Infinity Saga consists of the Phases 1, 2, and 3.

The Multiverse-Saga

The The Multiverse Saga will consist of the Phases 4, 5, and 6.
